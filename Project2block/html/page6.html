Step 6: Making a Schedule

After making the final draft, think about how often you can update your comic. Will it be once a week? Once a month? Once every two months? That’ll be up to you to determine. Once you’ve got an idea of how often you’ll update, think about how large your buffer will be. Most comics have more than one page done at a time in order to allow for a constant flow of content, or in the case that a page can’t be done on time due to various circumstances. This is what we call a buffer, and how large your buffer will be will also vary.
